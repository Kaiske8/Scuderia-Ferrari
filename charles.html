Biography

Born in Monaco on 16 October 1997, Charles grew up on the streets that would one day become his greatest triumph. He began karting at four, coached by his father Hervé — himself a former racing driver — who pushed him to pursue perfection from an early age.

Tragedy struck in 2017 when his father passed away just days before Charles competed in the Baku F2 round, which he went on to win. His godfather was Jules Bianchi, the Ferrari development driver who lost his life following the 2014 Japanese Grand Prix. Leclerc carries both losses with him on track.

In 2019 Ferrari paired him with four-time champion Sebastian Vettel. Leclerc outperformed his teammate decisively, winning back-to-back in Spa and Monza in front of the tifosi — cementing his place in Ferrari folklore.
