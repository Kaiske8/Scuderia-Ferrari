Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton is a British racing driver who has won seven Formula 1 World Championships, tying the record held by Michael Schumacher. Known for his aggressive driving style and exceptional skill on the track, Hamilton has become one of the most successful and influential figures in motorsport history.
